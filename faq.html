Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First, you need to go to https://hzuniversity.topdesk.net/tas/public/ssp/content/detail/knowledgeitem?unid=d065a4e8a56d47dcbd0ffb9cdf78f20b&origin=searchPreview, log into your account, and after that, you can upload all the files you want to print. After choosing the file, you can set up a couple of more things and confirm your request. Next, you go to the printer (for example on the first floor next to the staircase), scan your card, choose the printing option, and your documents should be printed.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Visit https://hzuniversity.topdesk.net/tas/public/ssp/content/detail/knowledgeitem?unid=d065a4e8a56d47dcbd0ffb9cdf78f20b&origin=searchPreview and log in.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

Go to the Webshop and there you have to log in to your HZ account. After that, you can purchase whatever you want, and you can pay via PayPal.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

You can book it from the HZ Self Service Portal. When you enter you can go to 'Make a reservation', after that 'Reserve meeting room or Project Group room' and 'Reserve a room' and then you can choose a room.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You will be required to show your HZ ID at the gate. After doing so, you will be allowed to park your car.
