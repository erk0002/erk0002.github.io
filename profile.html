Who am I?

About Me

I'm Tristan Martijn van Erk, a Dutch-American born in Atlanta, Georgia where I lived most of my life. My background as a Dutch-American has always been a source of pride and has shaped my perspective on life. In 2022, I embarked on an exciting new adventure when I moved to Belgium. There, I dedicated myself to my education, completing high school while undertaking the challenging International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) with Physics, Math AA, and Computer Science, all at the higher level. This academic journey has laid a solid foundation for my future endeavours, and I look forward to continuing to explore the world and all the opportunities it has to offer.
